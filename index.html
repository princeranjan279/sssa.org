About SSS Academy

SSS Academy is a premier shooting facility dedicated to providing a safe and welcoming environment for sport shooters, law enforcement, and families alike. Our team brings years of expertise, ensuring every player receives professional guidance, whether you’re here for training, or practice.

  • Modern range with 28+ Fire Arms Shooting Lanes
  • Top-tier ventilation and safety measures
  • Private lessons, group events
